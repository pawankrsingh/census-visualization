Are economic development and air quality inversely related?

Industries are a great source of both pollution and wealth for states. They help generate employments and are a big source of income for governments, however, they are also responsible for tons of waste products such as chemical fluids and toxic fumes that are let out into the environment, leading to low quality of air and other health complications. Power plants also play a big role in industrial pollution and together with the heavy industries they account for nearly 80% of all greenhouse gas emissions. Some states are known to be industrial states and have a number of big and robust industries.
