How to reduce crime rates on countries and states?

Crime rates are one of the most complex indexes on a society. Crime rates on each country or city are influenced by the most diverse factors, from social disparity to employment rates. To shed light on some of these impacting factors, we have two charts for crime data. On the first one, gun ownership is compared against the homidice rates on some states of US, it is important to note that the number of owned guns in this chart just take into account properly registered guns, excluding guns used illegally. The secon chart, makes clear the relation between crime rates and educational level on several countries in the world.
